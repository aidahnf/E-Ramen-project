About Us

established since 1910 in Japan and brought to Indonesia since 2015.

ERamen shop is one of the oldest ramen shops in the world. All ERamen menu made by organic flour and turn into delicious ramen noodle.

Customer can find any kind of ramen, such as ramen and so many kind of tempura. Today, Eramen has many outlets that are spread in almost all regions in Indonesia.

ERamen committed to providing the best service for our customers and opened all outlet with No Pork No Lard

ERamen served both beef and chicken ramen in all outlets.

ERamen aim to Create Memorable Experiences and delight people with unique menus.
